Section 08

SoftWave

Purple appointment block · Call to confirm · Deposit required

⚠ Call only SoftWave confirmations = CALL ONLY. Use the Google Docs SW script. Do not text.

🌷 Room Setup

  • Verify deposit was collected
  • Open patient chart — confirm treatment area
  • Select correct video for treatment area Back · Neck · Shoulder · Knee
  • Start video BEFORE patient enters room
  • SET TIMER — videos do NOT stop automatically
  • Confirm timer is running before leaving room
⚠ Always set the timer No timer = random / inappropriate videos may play.

🌷 During Visit

  • Prepare room
  • Call patient to confirm appointment, treatment area, deposit / payment, and cancellation policy
  • Document the confirmation call
  • Stop video when timer ends

🌷 Pulses & Plan

  • Discovery / first visit may include about 500 pulses
  • Typical recommendation can be 4, 6, or 8 sessions depending on pain severity and area
  • Typical treatment may be around 600-800 pulses depending on area
  • $25 deposit required for SoftWave discovery

🌷 Cancellation — SoftWave

🌸 24 business hours notice required

Late cancel / no-show fee: $50
Saturday call for Monday = ✓ OK
Sunday call for Monday = × NOT OK
Same day = try to reschedule
Future cancel = delete & rebook

🌷 Cancellation — Adjustment

🌸 2 hours notice required

Inside 2 hours = $25 fee
Same day = try to reschedule
Future cancel = delete & rebook

⚠ Deposit reminder SoftWave appointments require a deposit collected ahead of the visit. Verify before setting up the room.

🌷 Aftercare

  • No ice, Advil, or ibuprofen after treatment
  • Avoid intense exercise for 2-3 days; light activity is okay
  • Relief may begin in 15-20 hours
  • Healing continues over 8-12 weeks
  • Schedule next SoftWave before patient leaves
