Section 03

New Patient Phone Call

NP Days: Mon · Wed · Fri  ·  Max 4–5 appts per day · 1× NPs at once

🌸 Golden Rule Smile before you answer. They can feel it through the phone. 🌸
💬 Answer

Café of Life Chiropractic, this is Valeria. How can I help you?

🌷 What This Call Is For

🌸 Goal This call turns a new lead into a scheduled Day 1 visit. Your job is to collect the right info, offer two appointment options, send forms, and set them up so Day 1 is smooth.
  • Get the green NP intake form ready before asking questions.
  • Collect patient info, reason for visit, referral source, and accident / injury details if applicable.
  • Offer two appointment options only; do not ask open-ended scheduling questions.
  • Create the patient in CT after the call.
  • Send intake forms, welcome email, and Review Wave app link.
  • File the green card so Day 1 is ready.
1Grab the bright green NP intake form
2Fill out the intake form while speaking to patient
  • Full name
  • Date of birth
  • Phone number
  • Email address
  • Referral — how did they hear about us?
  • Reason for visit
  • Accident or injury?  →  If YES: Insurance carrier · Claim number · Adjuster name · Adjuster phone
3Schedule the appointment
⚠ Never open-ended Never ask open-ended questions. Always offer exactly TWO options.
💬 Scheduling — Two Options

Would morning or afternoon work better for you?

We have Monday or Wednesday — which works?

I have ___ AM/PM or ___ AM/PM — which do you prefer?

4After hanging up
  • Create patient in ChiroTouch
  • Send intake forms
  • Send welcome email
  • Send app link via Review Wave
  • File the green intake card
