Section 03

New Patient Phone Call

NP Days: Mon · Wed · Fri  ·  Max 4–5 appts per day · 1× NPs at once

🌸 Golden Rule Smile before you answer. They can feel it through the phone. 🌸
💬 Answer

Café of Life Chiropractic, this is Valeria. How can I help you?

🌷 Quick Text Templates

Review Wave - New Lead

Hi [Name], here is the intake link for your new patient history. Please complete it before 9am the day of your appointment so we can be ready for you.

🌷 What This Call Is For

🌸 Goal This call turns a new lead into a scheduled Day 1 visit. Your job is to collect the right info, offer two appointment options, send forms, and set them up so Day 1 is smooth.
  • Get the green NP intake form ready before asking questions.
  • Collect patient info, reason for visit, referral source, and accident / injury details if applicable.
  • Offer two appointment options only; do not ask open-ended scheduling questions.
  • Create the patient in CT after the call.
  • Send intake forms, welcome email, and Review Wave app link.
  • File the green card so Day 1 is ready.
1Grab the bright green NP intake form
2Fill out the intake form while speaking to patient
  • Full name
  • Date of birth
  • Phone number
  • Email address
  • Referral — how did they hear about us?
  • Reason for visit
  • Accident or injury?  →  If YES: Insurance carrier · Claim number · Adjuster name · Adjuster phone
3Schedule the appointment
⚠ Never open-ended Never ask open-ended questions. Always offer exactly TWO options.
💬 Scheduling — Two Options

Would morning or afternoon work better for you?

We have Monday or Wednesday — which works?

I have ___ AM/PM or ___ AM/PM — which do you prefer?

4After hanging up
  • Appt & person entered to schedule & ChiroTouch
  • Scan intake card to NP file in CT
  • Send welcome email
  • Send intake forms / app link via Review Wave
  • Appointment confirmation call — note Confirmed and initials in CT

🌷 Confirmation Call Scripts

⚠ Script rule Make sure you do not abbreviate. Say ALL in script.

New Patient Confirmation Call

Hi! This is [your name] calling from Dr. Hastings Office at Cafe of Life Chiropractic. Is [patient name] there? I am calling to confirm your appointment on [day and time]. Do you need directions to our Pembroke Pines office? Please remember to fill out your new patient history at the intake link you got via email and text. We need that information filled before 9am the day of your appointment. Thank you! We look forward to meeting you!

Dr Report Confirmation Call

Hello, this is [your name] calling from Cafe of Life Chiropractic office. Is [patient name] there? I was calling to confirm your appointment for the Doctor's report on [day and time]. Great! We look forward to seeing you on [day and time]. If you need to make any appointment changes, please remember our 24 hour appointment policy. Thank you!

🌷 Missed NP Appointment Action

  • Call and text 15 minutes after missed appointment
  • Call end of day after 6 pm
  • If no answer / no voicemail, send email
  • Call next office days
  • Call again in two weeks
