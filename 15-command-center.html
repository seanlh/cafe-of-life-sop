Command Center

Live shift tracker for a CA working solo

🌷 Start of Shift

1. Walk In / Clock In

  • Walk in and clock in
  • Put belongings away and get settled at the front desk
  • Open the Command Center for today

2. Systems First

  • Open CT
  • Open Review Wave
  • Open Cash Practice
  • Open email
  • Check voicemail

3. Patient Safety / Schedule Review

  • Review today's schedule for NP, ROF, Day 3, SoftWave, re-exams, and special cases
  • Check red letters and alerts before patients arrive
  • Check balances, ledgers, and open payment issues
  • Write any must-handle items in Pre-Shift Huddle / cannot forget

4. Money / Admin Processes

  • Check Cash Practice for red / declined items
  • Check money report or cash log needs
  • Check deposits needed for NP or SoftWave
  • Note any special pricing or balance conversations in patient card

5. Organizing / Cleaning Duties

  • Confirm rooms are ready for the first patients
  • Confirm forms, receipts, scanner, and phone are ready
  • Restock front desk / patient supplies if needed
  • Tidy front desk and patient-facing areas

🌷 Appointment Requirements

New Patient

Needs before visit:

If missing: call patient, note attempt in CT, add to Open Loops, and tell Dr. Jen / manager if it affects the visit.

Day 1 / First Visit

Before patient leaves:

If missing: stop before checkout is final, finish the missing item, then mark Done.

ROF / Day 2

Needs during ROF:

If undecided: write undecided, add follow-up, and update later when they choose.

Day 3

Setup checklist:

If not finished: put the exact missing item in Post-Shift Huddle so it cannot disappear.

SoftWave

Must be confirmed by call:

If missing: call before visit, document attempt, and flag in Open Loops.

Adjustment / Special Case

Quick safety sweep:

If something feels off: mark Waiting on Dr., write the exact question, and do not mark Done yet.

🌷 Live Shift Log

Use this for Dr. walk-ups, CT chat messages, front desk surprises, money questions, forms, and anything that needs to survive the rush.

🌷 Quick How-To Steps

Dr. walks patient up

  1. Pause and write the patient name in Live Shift Log.
  2. Write the exact instruction before doing anything else.
  3. Check payment, ledger, balance, alerts, and next appointment.
  4. If CT needs a note/color/red letter, mark it before Done.

CT chat comes in

  1. Log source as CT chat with patient/topic.
  2. Copy the action into Next action.
  3. Mark Waiting on Dr. / huddle if you need clarification.
  4. Do not clear it until the patient card or CT is updated.

Money or special pricing

  1. Check Cash Practice and patient ledger.
  2. Write the amount, card last 4, or special price detail.
  3. Mark green font / money issue if needed.
  4. Tell Dr. Jen / manager if it affects checkout.
