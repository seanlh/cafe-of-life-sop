🌷 Start of Shift

  • Open CT, Review Wave, Cash Practice, email, and voicemail
  • Review today's schedule for NP, ROF, Day 3, SoftWave, and re-exams
  • Check red letters, alerts, balances, and open payment issues before patients arrive
  • Confirm rooms, forms, receipts, scanner, phone, and front desk supplies are ready

🌷 Before Any Patient Leaves

  • Payment handled
  • Ledger checked
  • Balance checked
  • Alerts checked
  • Next appointment scheduled
  • Date and time said out loud
  • Receipt signed, scanned, and shredded if applicable
  • Red-letter action items completed

🌷 End-of-Day Sweep

  • Every patient reviewed
  • Payments handled
  • Next appointments scheduled
  • Receipts scanned and shredded
  • Review Wave checked
  • Voicemail checked
  • Email checked
  • Money report / cash log handled
  • Tomorrow's NP / ROF / SW checked
