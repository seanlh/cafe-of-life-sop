Day 2 — ROF

Report of Findings · red appointment block · $55 ROF fee

🌷 Day 2 ROF Workflow

🌸 Red block = report of findings Day 2 is the care plan and financial decision visit. Collect the ROF fee first, support the doctor's presentation, then complete the care plan, payments, future schedule, and notes before the patient leaves.
  • Collect $55 ROF fee, print / sign / scan / shred receipt.
  • Pull Day 3 literature and questions packet.
  • Doctor reviews X-rays, findings, and care plan.
  • Patient signs care plan before leaving, even if undecided.
  • Set up PIF, monthly, PPV, or document undecided.
  • Schedule future visits, re-exam, exercise consult, and Day 3.
  • Complete ROF / care plan notes and alert packages the same day.

🌷 Before ROF Begins

  • Collect $55 ROF payment
  • Print receipt · patient signs · scan · shred
  • Pull Day 3 literature & questions packet
  • Pull Day 1 & 3 lit for spouse if applicable

🌷 Doctor — During ROF

  • Doctor reviews X-ray analysis
  • Doctor reviews findings with patient
  • Doctor presents care plan

🌷 After ROF — Front Office

  • Collect care plan payment · print / sign / scan / shred receipt
⚠ Must sign care plan Patient MUST sign care plan before leaving. Even if hesitant — sign today acknowledging they received it.
  • Patient signs care plan
  • Set up PIF or auto debit
  • Schedule future visits
  • Schedule Re-Exam
  • Schedule Exercise Consult
  • Schedule Day 3
  • Print schedule copy for patient
  • Give 2 health passes (7-day expiration)
  • Discuss spouse / kids appointment (if applicable)
  • Offer spouse exam / X-rays today
💬 Goodbye

See you at your next appointment on DATE + TIME!

🌷 After ROF — Back Office

  • Review care plan and financials
  • Complete ROF note
  • Set up alert packages — same day as ROF
  • Change payment type in CT
  • Send NP history email for family
  • Send app link via Review Wave
