Section 05

Day 2 — ROF

Report of Findings · red appointment block · $55 ROF fee

🌷 Best Order: Day 2 ROF Workflow

🌸 Red block = report of findings Day 2 is the care plan and financial decision visit. Collect the ROF fee first, use the handout/video flow, then complete the care plan, payments, future schedule, notes, alerts, and records.
  • Collect $55 ROF fee, print / sign / scan / shred receipt.
  • Pull Day 3 Literature & Questions.
  • Pull Day 1 & 3 Lit & Questions for spouse if applicable.
  • Doctor reviews X-rays, findings, and care plan.
  • Patient signs care plan before leaving, even if undecided.
  • Document Offered and Commitment: PPV, PIF, monthly, or undecided.
  • Schedule patient appointments, re-exam, exercise consult, and Day 3.
  • Complete ROF note, alert packages, payment type, and scan/import records same day.

🌷 Before ROF Begins

  • Take ROF payment $55
  • Print receipt · patient signs · scan · shred
  • Day 3 Literature & Questions
  • Day 1 & 3 Lit & Questions — spouse

💬 Video setup

We're going to show you a brief video that will review what normal versus abnormal x-rays look like. Please turn off your cell phone and feel free to take some notes, as the doctor will be referencing back to this information when reviewing your x-rays.

  • Doctor reviews findings with patient
