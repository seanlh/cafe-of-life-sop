🌷 Office Rules

  • Work ahead. Every downtime = prep time.
  • Next appointment scheduled before every patient leaves.
  • Charge before services rendered — always.
  • Pick up phone within 2 rings.
  • Always offer two scheduling options — never open-ended.
  • Reply to all messages / texts same day. (Do NOT archive Review Wave messages.)
  • Initial and date every note in CT.
  • New patient: no CT PIN yet — Dr. checks them in.
  • Established patient = italics in CT.
  • Red letters in CT = address before visit.
  • Angry patient → huddle with Dr. Jen.
  • No Medicare. No Medicaid.
  • Seminole & UHC = in-network.
  • Billing questions → billing dept (Tues & Thurs).
  • POA / ROI / Insurance Agent = do NOT give out patient info.
  • Written cancellation = written consent required.
