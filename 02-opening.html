Section 02

Opening Procedures

First hour before patients arrive

1. Office / Supplies

  • Clock in.
  • Lights and Alarm — OFF
  • Turn on Alexa, TVs / chiro info videos, and diffuser
  • Tidy office and clean floors / carpet / common surfaces as needed — Tue, Thu, Fri deep clean
  • Restock paper towels, toilet paper, hand soap, exercise area, SW room, and face paper
  • Turn AC to 72 degrees prior to patients coming in
  • Queue SoftWave videos or timers if needed
⚠ Do not water plants Water plants only if no one is designated. Usually 2x per week; ask Dr. Jen if being done Wednesday & Saturday.

2. Systems / Schedule

  • Turn on computers and log in check-in station
  • Turn on X-Ray computer only if X-ray machine is needed
  • Fill out Google Sheet "Cash Log" — enter N/A under total payments received
  • Upload Cash Practice Payments
  • Check patient files with balances & correct
  • Check all red / declined payments in ledger
  • Check voicemail, office email, Google Calendar, and Review Wave Text Service
  • Return patient voicemail calls / texts
  • Call missed appointments — done within 15 minutes of scheduled time
  • Review today's schedule for NP, ROF, Day 3, SW, re-exams, and special appointments
  • Enter appointments into the Daily Shift Tracker
  • Check ledgers, balances, alerts, red letters, and future appointments
  • Prepare patient charts — NP, re-exams, ROF, special appointments
🌸 If unsure Anything looks off → ask Dr. Jen before clearing it. Do not archive messages until they are handled.
