Section 02

Opening Procedures

Do all of this before the first patient arrives

1. Clock In & Open Systems

  • Clock in.
  • Lights on, computers on, check-in station logged in.
  • Open CT, Review Wave, Cash Practice, email, and voicemail.
  • Turn on X-Ray computer if X-rays or reviews are expected.

2. Patient Risk / Day Map

  • Review today's schedule for NP, Day 1, ROF, Day 3, SoftWave, re-exams, and special cases.
  • Mon / Wed / Fri: watch New Patient and Day 1 confirmations, deposits, and intake forms.
  • Tue / Thu afternoon: watch ROF / Day 2, $55 fee, care plan, and payment setup.
  • Write red letters, alerts, balances, special pricing, and doctor instructions into the huddle sheet.

3. Money / Admin Processes

  • Check Cash Practice for red or declined payments.
  • Import payments into ChiroTouch and verify the patient ledger.
  • Check cash log / money report needs.
  • Verify balances match and write questions before checkout happens.
🌸 If unsure Anything looks off → ask Dr. Jen before clearing it.

4. Communications

  • Check voicemail and write down every message.
  • Return patient voicemail calls / texts.
  • Check office email and reply to same-day messages.
  • Check Review Wave messages and missed calls.
🌸 Note Do not archive messages until they are handled.

5. Rooms / Supplies / Cleaning

  • Prepare rooms and fresh face paper on every table.
  • Confirm forms, receipts, scanner, phone, and front desk supplies are ready.
  • Queue SoftWave videos or timers if needed.
  • Left TV → Italy / Scenic / Travel / Relaxing; other TVs → ADJ Room Playlist.
  • AC → 72°F; diffuser 3–4 drops away from plants.
  • Sweep floors (Tuesday · Thursday · Friday only).
⚠ Do not water plants Dr. Jen and Aunt handle Wed & Sat. Ask first.
