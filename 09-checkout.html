Section 09

Checkout — Every Visit

Three sections · Check · Collect · Schedule

🌷 Section 1 — Check

Open ChiroTouch. Click on patient.

  • Check notes
  • Check alerts
  • Check balance
  • Check ledger
  • Check future appointments
  • Read any red letters — address anything flagged

🌷 Section 2 — Collect

⚠ Always Charge BEFORE services rendered.
  • Collect payment if due
  • Print receipt
  • Patient signs receipt
  • Scan receipt into patient file
  • Shred after confirmed scan
  • Offer copy to patient

🌷 Section 3 — Schedule

⚠ No exceptions No patient leaves without their next appointment scheduled.
  • Schedule NEXT appointment
  • Confirm date and time OUT LOUD with patient
  • Confirm it is in ChiroTouch

🌷 Final Complete

⚠ Patient is not DONE until all are complete Payment, ledger, balance, alerts, receipt, next appointment, date/time confirmation, and red-letter action items must all be handled before the patient leaves.
  • Payment handled
  • Ledger checked
  • Balance checked
  • Alerts checked
  • Receipt signed, scanned, and shredded if applicable
  • Next appointment scheduled
  • Date and time confirmed out loud
  • Red-letter action items completed
