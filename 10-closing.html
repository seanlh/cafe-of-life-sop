Section 10

Closing Procedures

Do all of this after the last patient leaves

🌷 Communications

  • Check voicemail — write down every message
  • Check office email — reply
  • Check Review Wave — reply to all texts
  • Reply to all messages / texts
🌸 Note Do NOT archive. Reply same day.

🌷 End of Day Tasks

  • Call missed appointments within 15 min of scheduled time
  • Confirm tomorrow's appointments: NP · ROF · SW · Day 3 · Re-Acts
  • Fill out Cash Log — enter N/A under total payments received
  • Super-bills finished and filed — note dates of any missing SOAPs
  • Email Dr. Jen call list + next 3 business days schedule

🌷 Cash

  • Print Money Report → give to Dr. Jen
  • Count cash in cash box → Dr. Jen signs receipt → fill out cash log
🌸 Deposit reminder NP = deposit. SW = deposit. ROF = NO deposit.

🌷 Chart Prep for Tomorrow

  • Pull charts for tomorrow's patients
  • Organize by appointment time — earliest on top
  • Flag NPs · ROFs · SoftWaves · Re-Exams

🌷 Cleaning

  • Take out garbage — daily
  • Clean adjustment tables + conditioner
  • Clean rooms and monitors

🌷 Lock Up

  • TVs off
  • Lights off
  • Set alarm → AWAY
⚠ Last out Double-check the alarm panel is set to AWAY before locking the door.
