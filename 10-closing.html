Section 10

Closing Procedures

End-of-day order from last patient to alarm set

🌷 Closing the Day

  • Check voicemail, office email, Review Wave Text Service, RW messages, and missed calls — write down anything not done
  • Fill out Google Sheet "Cash Log" — enter N/A under total payments received
  • Call missed appointments — done within 15 minutes of their scheduled time
  • Confirm: NP, ROF, SW, Day 3 & RE-Acts
  • Review with Dr. Jen any problems or questions during huddles
  • Make sure all super-bills have been finished and filed away; write down dates of missing SOAPs
🌸 Note Check voicemail, email, Review Wave, missed calls, and missed appointments before you leave.

🌷 Next-Day Prep

  • Prepare patient charts for next day — NP, re-exams, ROF, SW SOAPs
  • Email Dr. Jen call list with date of service, NP / ROF / schedule info, and schedule for next 3 business days

🌷 Cash

  • Print Money Report — give $ to Dr. Jen
  • Count cash in the cash box and give it to Dr. Jen
  • Have Dr. Jen sign that she received cash
  • Fill out Google Sheet "cash log"
🌸 Deposit reminder NP = deposit. SW = deposit. ROF = NO deposit.

🌷 Room Reset

  • Garbage — daily
  • Clean adjustment tables / conditioner, rooms, and monitors

🌷 Turn Off

  • Turn off lights, Alexa, and TVs

🌷 Lock Up

  • Set Alarm — AWAY
⚠ Last out Double-check the alarm panel is set to AWAY before locking the door.
