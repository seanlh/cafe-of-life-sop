🌷 Day 3 Workflow

🌸 Burgundy block = onboarding complete Day 3 finishes the patient's setup after they accept care. Make sure payment automation, PIN, app, schedule, and policy understanding are complete before they leave.
  • Open Cash Practice and prepare EZ-Pay authorization.
  • Have patient choose credit card or ACH and sign EZ-Pay.
  • File signed EZ-Pay form and confirm payment setup.
  • Assign CT PIN and show patient their subjective / self check-in flow.
  • Send app link via Review Wave and help them get set up.
  • Schedule appointments 1-6 months out and print schedule copy.
  • Review cancellation policies and fees before they walk out.
