Day 1 — First Visit

New patient · Day 1 checklist · green appointment block

🌷 Best Order: Day 1 Workflow

🌸 Green block = first visit Day 1 is where the patient arrives for their first in-office visit. Work the NP checklist in order: front office, back office, then ROF setup before they leave.
  • Meet & Greet NP.
  • Copy insurance card & photo ID.
  • Give Day 1 Handout & Q's.
  • Take payment before services.
  • Take photo of patient and complete file in CT.
  • Give patient gift bag and office tour.
  • Confirm NP appointment, schedule ROF, give ROF handout, and put file in ROF holder.

🌷 Front Office

When they arrive:

  • Meet & Greet NP
  • Copy insurance card & photo ID
  • Day 1 Handout & Q's

Payment — BEFORE they go back:

⚠ Always Charge before services. Always.
  • Check ledger, balance, alerts, and card on file
  • Take Payment
  • Print receipt
  • Patient signs receipt
  • Scan receipt into patient file + shred

After visit — before they leave:

  • Take Photo of Patient
  • Complete File in CT — referred from?
  • Pt gift bag
  • Office Tour
⚠ Non-negotiable Schedule ROF before patient leaves.
  • Schedule ROF & ROF Handout
  • Confirm ROF date and time out loud before patient leaves

🌷 Back Office

During visit:

  • Patient sign the 3 Sheets
  • Confirm NP Appointment
  • Verify Insurance
  • Dr. First Call Sheet
  • Schedule ROF & ROF Handout
  • Put File in ROF Holder
