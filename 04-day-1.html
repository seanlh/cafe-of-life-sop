Section 04

Day 1 — First Visit

New patient · green appointment block

🌷 Best Order: Day 1 Workflow

🌸 Green block = first visit Day 1 is where the patient arrives for their first in-office visit. Handle ID / insurance, payment, forms, gift bag / tour, and make sure the ROF is scheduled before they leave.
  • Welcome patient by name and collect / copy ID and insurance.
  • Give Day 1 handout and questions.
  • Check balance, card on file, and collect payment before services.
  • Print receipt, get signature, scan, and shred.
  • Take patient photo, complete CT file, and note referral source.
  • Give gift bag and office tour.
  • Schedule Day 2 ROF, give ROF handout, and say the date / time out loud.

🌷 Front Office

When they arrive:

  • Greet by name — warm welcome
  • Copy insurance card
  • Copy photo ID
  • Hand Day 1 handout & Q's

Payment — BEFORE they go back:

⚠ Always Charge before services. Always.
  • Check balance in CT
  • Check card on file
  • Ask: "We have a card on file ending in ____. Is it okay to use that today?"
  • Charge patient
  • Print receipt
  • Patient signs receipt
  • Scan receipt into patient file + shred

After visit — before they leave:

  • Take patient photo in ChiroTouch
  • Complete file in CT — note referral source
  • Give gift bag
  • Give office tour
⚠ Non-negotiable Schedule ROF before patient leaves.
  • Schedule ROF (Day 2)
  • Give ROF handout
  • Confirm ROF date and time out loud before patient leaves

🌷 Back Office

During visit:

  • Patient signs the 3 sheets
  • Confirm NP appointment in CT
  • Verify insurance
  • Dr. First Call Sheet completed
  • Schedule ROF + give ROF handout
  • Put file in ROF holder

🌷 ROF Days

Tuesday · Thursday
Afternoons only
